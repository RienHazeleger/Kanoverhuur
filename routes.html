Urban Architecture Route

Vathorst is one of the prettiest new neighbourhoods in The Netherlands. Its bustling and versatile architecture is a true feast to the eye. People come to live here for its central and convenient location, its beautiful canals and romantic atmosphere.

The architects' motto was to create "A World of Difference", and they definitely succeeded! Areas such as De Laak, Waterdorp and the Laakse Tuinen each offer their own stunning architecture. Now, what better way to experience this than from the water?

Arkemheen Nature Route

The Arkemheen Polder is a protected Natura2000 area with rare plants and animals. The area is critically important to meadow birds such as godwits, lapwings and wigeons. An endless perspective, painted with grass, water and clouds in a typical Dutch sky.

While your kayak silently glides through the water of the Laak river, swallows accompany you through the narrow bends of the oldest polder area in the Netherlands. No wonder they call this one of Hollands 20 most beautiful landscapes.
