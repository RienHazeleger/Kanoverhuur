How to get there

Due to the fact that we’re located in an urban area, there is limited public parking space available. We cannot guarantee parking immediately close to our premises, but chances are high you’ll find a space within 100m of our location. We encourage you to come by bike or public transport.

Car:

Amersfoort Vathorst is easily accessible from the highway A28 (exit 8A, Amersfoort Vathorst) and highway A1 (exit 13, Amersfoort Noord). From there, follow the signs to Vathorst and exit the circular road at roundabout #7 and follow the road for 300 meters. Google Maps helps you to get to our location.

Train:

From Amersfoort Central Station, take the sprinter to stop at Amersfoort Vathorst. From the station, it’s 700 meters to our location. You can plan your train trip at the NS website.

Bus:

From Amersfoort Central Station: take bus 3, 5 or 7 and leave the bus at the Damespolder stop. From there, it’s 300 meters to our location. Plan your trip at OV9292 or with Syntus.
