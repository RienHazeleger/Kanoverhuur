Eénpersoons Kayaks

Our Rainbow Oasis 4.30 is a very stable sea kayak. It’s easy to use and very reliable for daytrips and the lake and flatwater excursions that we offer. It comes standard with an elastic rope, an adjustable back band, adjustable ergonomic seat and a touring footrest. This kayak is ideal for people with little to no experience.

Tweepersoons Kayaks

Our WAVEsport Vista 485 is a fast and efficient open cockpit tandem kayak. The spacious cockpit provides room for two adults and a small passenger or a pet. It’s an exceptionally popular touring tandem, with easy handling combined with great speed and stability. A rear compartment allows to bring along and conveniently store luggage.

Canadese (familie) Kano's

Our Pelican 14.6DLX features 3 seats, of which 2 comfortable folding seats and a built-in cooler seat in the center. For convenience, rod holders, drink holders and carrying handles are also featured. This Canadian is easy to handle while providing plenty of room for family, friends and fishing gear. Made of RAM-X™ impact-resistant material, it is built to last.
